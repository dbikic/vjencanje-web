Questions & Answers

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive 15-30 minutes before the ceremony begins. The ceremony starts at 4:00 PM.

What is the dress code?

We'd love to see our family and friends dressed up for our special day! Cocktail attire is preferred. Please avoid wearing white.

Can I bring a plus one?

Please check your invitation for the number of guests included. If you have any questions, please contact us directly.

Will the ceremony and reception be at the same location?

Yes, both the ceremony and reception will be held at the same venue. There will be a cocktail hour between the ceremony and reception.

Is there parking available?

Yes, parking is available on-site at the venue. Please follow the signs upon arrival.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Please let us know about any dietary restrictions or allergies when you RSVP, and we'll make sure to accommodate your needs.

Are children welcome?

While we love your little ones, we've decided to have an adults-only celebration. We appreciate your understanding!

What happens if it rains?

The venue has both indoor and outdoor spaces. In case of rain, the ceremony will be moved indoors. We'll keep you updated!
